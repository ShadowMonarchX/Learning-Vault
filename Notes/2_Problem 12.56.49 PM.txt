# How to Identify & Deeply Research Your Startup Problem for High Pre-Seed Funding

---

## 1. Define Your Problem Scope Precisely

* Start with your broad problem area, e.g., **business workflow overload in SMBs**.
* Break it down into **specific pain points** that cause the biggest friction. Examples:

  * CRM data errors & manual entry waste hours daily
  * Sales teams spend 30%+ time on admin vs selling
  * Managers lack real-time workflow visibility causing delays

---

## 2. Quantify the Problem with Data

* Collect **quantitative data**:

  * Time wasted on manual workflow tasks (surveys, reports)
  * Financial losses from inefficiencies (e.g., delayed sales, errors)
  * Market size numbers from reports (Gartner, McKinsey, Forrester)
* This will build your **business case and market size**, which investors want.

---

## 3. Conduct Deep Customer Interviews & Surveys

* Interview **30‚Äì50 SMB owners, sales managers, knowledge workers** across industries.
* Prepare **detailed interview questions**:

  * How much time do you spend on repetitive workflows?
  * What tools do you currently use? Pain points?
  * What would an ideal AI assistant do to help?
  * How much would you pay for such a solution?
* Record pain stories, unmet needs, and willingness to pay.

---

## 4. Analyze Competitors & Alternatives

* List all current solutions: automation tools, CRM plugins, AI assistants.
* Identify **gaps where they fail** (complexity, cost, lack of AI-driven intelligence).
* Show why your AI agent approach is uniquely positioned to solve these.

---

## 5. Define Your Unique Value Proposition (UVP)

* Based on the research, craft a sharp UVP:
  *‚ÄúFlowMate AI saves SMB sales teams 10+ hours/week by automating CRM updates, reports, and follow-ups ‚Äî without coding or costly consultants.‚Äù*
* This should address the biggest pain you validated.

---

## 6. Build a Research Report / Pitch Appendix

* Summarize:

  * Market size & growth trends
  * Customer pain points and quotes
  * Competitor analysis & differentiation
  * Financial impact (time & money saved)
* This adds credibility and confidence for investors.

---

## 7. Prepare Your Pre-Seed Pitch

* Problem + market + solution + traction (prototype/user feedback) + go-to-market plan
* Highlight your deep research, unique insights, and why \$2M+ is needed now to build scalable AI & integrations.
